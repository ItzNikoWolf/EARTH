## Theorem (R3-THREE)  
Alexander T. Rayman, Richard Vaught  

Physical space is exactly three-dimensional because the self-generating golden braid that forms the vacuum requires precisely **three** strands to remain:

- infinite in extent  
- non-self-intersecting at all finite generations

Three strands is the unique solution that satisfies all four conditions simultaneously.
